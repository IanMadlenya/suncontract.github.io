SOLAR ENERGY:

Energy is the key driver of all economies; even cryptocurrencies and blockchain technologies depend on energy. In the long run it is crucial for any community to be energy self-sufficient. The Sun, as a primary, clean, and abundant energy source, gives us opportunity to create an independent decentralized autonomous organization.


BLOCKCHAIN

technology can be utilised to connect renewable energy producers, investors and energy consumers. It is the combination of solar power plants and Ethereum network that enables DAO stakeholders to buy and sell clean energy from each other securely and automatically, using smart contracts.


GREEN INVESTMENT:

The Ethereum blockchain enables anyone to participate in building a self sustainable future. Powerdao connects people from all around the world to participate, either with a micro investment or a large sum.

The Vision - Decentralized, Smart, Green

The vision is to connect Independent Power Producers (IPPs) and Consumers and create global self-sufficient energy community based on renewables.
